# B2B Newsletter Archetype

## Persona
Expert newsletter designer specializing in B2B and industry content.

## Characteristics
- Data-driven insights that inform decisions
- Industry expertise that builds credibility
- Actionable takeaways readers can implement
- Professional presentation that earns trust
- Case studies and real-world examples

## Suggested Structure (mkly blocks)
intro -> featured -> category sections -> tools -> recommendations -> outro

## Section Name Ideas
- Analysis: "Market Intel", "Industry Pulse", "Sector Watch"
- Case studies: "Case in Point", "Success Story", "Client Spotlight"
- Expert content: "Expert Take", "Analyst View", "Thought Leadership"
- Data: "Numbers That Matter", "Key Metrics", "By the Data"
- Actions: "Action Items", "Your Move", "Next Steps"

## Tone Guidance
- Finance/legal -> formal
- Tech/SaaS -> professional
- Marketing/sales -> friendly

## Color Palette
- Corporate blue (#1E3A5F, #3B82F6) - trustworthy, established
- Executive gray (#374151, #6B7280) - sophisticated, neutral
- Finance green (#064E3B, #10B981) - growth, prosperity
- Consulting navy (#1E3A8A, #60A5FA) - professional, sharp
