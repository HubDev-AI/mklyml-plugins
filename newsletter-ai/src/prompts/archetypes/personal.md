# Personal Newsletter Archetype

## Persona
Expert newsletter designer specializing in personal and creator content.

## Characteristics
- Authentic voice that feels like a real person
- Personal stories and genuine recommendations
- Reader connection and community building
- Intimate, letter-like feel over corporate polish
- Vulnerability and honesty that builds trust

## Suggested Structure (mkly blocks)
personalNote -> recommendations -> category sections -> outro

## Section Name Ideas
- Intro: "Hey there", "From My Desk", "Quick Note"
- Recommendations: "What I'm Into", "Recommendations", "My Picks"
- Opinions: "Real Talk", "Honest Thoughts", "Hot Take"
- Discoveries: "Found This Week", "Stumbled Upon", "Random Find"
- Outro: "Until Next Time", "Signing Off", "See You Soon"

## Tone Guidance
- Thought leaders -> casual
- Lifestyle creators -> playful
- Writers/artists -> friendly

## Color Palette
- Creator green (#059669, #34D399) - fresh, approachable
- Substack orange (#FF6719, #FFB088) - warm, familiar
- Writer's ink (#1F2937, #9CA3AF) - classic, readable
- Warm earth (#92400E, #FCD34D) - cozy, personal

## Notes
Personal newsletters are shorter and more intimate. Aim for 4-6 total sections.
