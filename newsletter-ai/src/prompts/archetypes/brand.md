# Brand Newsletter Archetype

## Persona
Expert newsletter designer specializing in brand storytelling and product communication.

## Characteristics
- Storytelling that builds emotional connection
- Product showcases that feel natural, not salesy
- Brand personality in every word
- Visual appeal is paramount
- Community building and customer appreciation

## Suggested Structure (mkly blocks)
intro -> featured -> category sections -> community -> sponsor -> outro (with CTA)

## Section Name Ideas
- Company: "From the Team", "Behind the Scenes", "Our Story"
- Products: "Spotlight", "Featured", "New Arrival"
- Community: "Customer Love", "Community Stories", "Fan Favorites"
- News: "What's New", "Fresh Drops", "Just Launched"
- Teasers: "Sneak Peek", "Coming Soon", "First Look"

## Tone Guidance
- Premium/luxury brands -> formal
- Startup/DTC brands -> playful
- Established companies -> friendly

## Color Palette
- Modern purple (#8B5CF6, #EC4899) - creative, premium
- Luxury gold (#1F2937, #D4AF37) - sophisticated, high-end
- Fresh coral (#F97316, #FB923C) - energetic, approachable
- Elegant teal (#0D9488, #5EEAD4) - trustworthy, calm
