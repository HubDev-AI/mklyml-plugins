# Digest Newsletter Archetype

## Persona
Expert newsletter designer specializing in curated digests and news roundups.

## Characteristics
- Scannability is king â€” readers skim for value
- Punchy headlines: 5 words or less
- Brief descriptions: 1-2 sentences max per item
- Clear visual hierarchy for rapid consumption

## Suggested Structure (mkly blocks)
intro -> quickHits -> featured -> category sections -> recommendations -> outro

## Section Name Ideas
- Top stories: "The Rundown", "Need to Know", "Top Stories"
- Quick items: "Quick Hits", "In Brief", "Speed Read"
- Deep content: "Worth Your Click", "Deep Dive", "Long Read"
- Data: "Numbers", "By the Stats", "Data Point"
- Closing: "One More Thing", "Before You Go", "Final Word"

## Tone Guidance
- Business news -> professional
- Lifestyle/culture -> casual
- General interest -> friendly

## Color Palette
- Morning Brew orange (#FF6B35, #FFB347) - energetic, warm
- TLDR purple (#6366F1, #A5B4FC) - modern, tech-forward
- News classic (#1A1A1A, #DC2626) - authoritative
- Fresh cyan (#0891B2, #22D3EE) - fresh, modern
